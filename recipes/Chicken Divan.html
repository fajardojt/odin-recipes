Chicken Divan

Description

Chicken Divan is a creamy, rich, savory casserole that features chicken, broccoli, and a cheesy sauce.

Read on for the instructions on how to make it yourself!

INGREDIENTS

  • 3 chicken breasts
  • 3 broccoli crowns
  • 2 cans cream of chicken
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 TBS lemon juice
  • 2 TBS curry powder
  • 1 bag shredded cheese
  • 3/4 cup bread crumbs
  • 2 TBS melted butter

STEPS

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Chop broccoli into bite-sized pieces and set aside.
  3. Cut chicken into small slices.
  4. Heat up a wok or large frying pan to medium and coat with a light layer of oil.
  5. Fry the chicken until fully cooked. Place into your casserole dish.
  6. Fry the broccoli until bright green and desired texture. Place into casserole dish and mix thoroughly with the chicken.
  7. Mix the cream of chicken, mayonnaise, lemon juice, and curry powder together. Evenly distribute on top of the chicken and broccoli.
  8. Cover the dish with the cheese. Mix the bread crumbs into the melted butter and sprinkle evenly across the dish.
  9. Bake for 25 minutes or until the cheese has bubbled. Serve on top of rice or noodles.
