Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

An American classic, this easy sandwich is so good you'll make five of them in one night.

Read on to learn how to make your own peanut butter and jelly sandwich!

INGREDIENTS

  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly
  • Bread

STEPS

  1. On one piece of bread, smear a generous portion of peanut butter.
  2. On a second piece of bread, smear a light portion of jelly. If you want a sweeter sandwich, smear more jelly.
  3. Put the two pieces of bread together.

TIP: If you are not eating your sandwich right away, spread peanut butter on both pieces of bread and put jelly on top of them. The peanut buter acts as a lipid layer that protects the bread from the watery jelly, preventing it from getting soggy.
